# Document Templates

This directory contains templates for various document types to ensure consistency and compliance with DCO certification standards.

## Available Templates

Templates will be added here for:

- Legal documents
- Governance documents
- Technical specifications
- Marketing materials
- Board meeting minutes
- Internal communications

## Template Structure

Each template includes:
- Required metadata fields
- Standard disclaimers
- Formatting guidelines
- Certification preparation checklist

## Article I: Department Identity

### 1.1 — Mission Statement

The Document Certification Office (DCO) is established to ensure all official documents, publications, and external communications of Strategickhaos DAO LLC bear the official NFT Stamp of Approval, maintaining brand integrity, legal authenticity, and sovereign verification standards.

### 1.2 — Core Responsibilities

```yaml
primary_functions:
  - certification: "Review and approve documents for official NFT stamp"
  - verification: "Validate document authenticity and compliance"
  - trademark_protection: "Safeguard the Ratio Ex Nihilo trademark"
  - quality_assurance: "Ensure documents meet DAO standards"
  - audit_trail: "Maintain cryptographic record of all certifications"
```

### 1.3 — Jurisdiction

The DCO oversees certification for:
- Legal and governance documents
- External communications and publications
- Marketing and branding materials
- Smart contract documentation
- API and technical specifications
- Partnership agreements
- Board meeting minutes and resolutions

## Article IV: Certification Workflow

### 4.1 — Standard Certification Process

```yaml
workflow_stages:
  
  stage_1_submission:
    action: "Author submits document for certification"
    location: "/governance/certification/pending/"
    required_metadata:
      - document_type
      - author_name
      - creation_date
      - requested_tier
      - purpose
    
  stage_2_intake_review:
    action: "Certification Reviewer performs initial assessment"
    checks:
      - "Format compliance"
      - "Required disclaimers present"
      - "Author attribution clear"
      - "Template adherence (if applicable)"
    duration: "24 hours"
    
  stage_3_ai_validation:
    action: "Multi-AI consensus verification"
    models:
      - "Claude 3.5 Sonnet (legal/governance)"
      - "GPT-4 (technical accuracy)"
      - "Grok (chaos testing/edge cases)"
      - "Llama 3 (compliance patterns)"
    consensus_required:
      platinum: "4/4"
      gold: "3/4"
      silver: "2/4"
    
  stage_4_compliance_check:
    action: "Compliance Validator reviews legal/regulatory aspects"
    verifications:
      - "No UPL violations"
      - "Wyoming DAO statute compliance"
      - "Appropriate disclaimers"
      - "Third-party rights respected"
    
  stage_5_approval_signature:
    action: "CCO or Deputy CCO approves and signs"
    platinum_requirements:
      - "CCO GPG signature required"
      - "OpenTimestamps blockchain anchor"
      - "Attorney sign-off (if legal document)"
    gold_requirements:
      - "Deputy CCO approval sufficient"
      - "SHA256 hash logged"
    
  stage_6_stamp_application:
    action: "NFT stamp embedded or attached to document"
    formats:
      html: "Embedded SVG with metadata"
      pdf: "Watermark overlay + metadata page"
      markdown: "Badge image + verification footer"
      yaml: "Certification block appended"
    metadata_included:
      - certification_tier
      - approval_date
      - approver_id
      - hash_sha256
      - timestamp_ots (if applicable)
      - gpg_signature (if applicable)
    
  stage_7_publication:
    action: "Certified document moved to official location"
    platinum: "/governance/certified/"
    gold: "/governance/approved/"
    silver: "/governance/drafts/"
    bronze: "/internal/"
```

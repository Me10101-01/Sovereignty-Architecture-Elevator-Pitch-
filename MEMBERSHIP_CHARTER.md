## ğŸ Bill Assistance Eligibility

### Who Qualifies

Contributing Members and above are eligible for bill assistance based on:

1. **Active Membership**: Must be in good standing
2. **Need Verification**: Income documentation or self-attestation
3. **Participation**: Encouraged but not mandatory (see hardship provisions)

### Priority System

Bills are paid in this order:
1. **Emergency Shutoffs**: Immediate risk of service termination
2. **Critical Utilities**: Electricity, gas, water
3. **Essential Services**: Internet (for remote work/education), phone
4. **By Submission Date**: FIFO for equal priority cases

### Limits

- No individual member limit (based on need)
- Pool limits apply (40% of surplus goes to member benefits)
- Emergency rent assistance available (limited allocation)
